Goals Added is a modeled expected-goals swing, not an official stat: disallowed goal = 1.00, converted penalty = 0.79 (standard conversion-rate value), red card = a rough time-remaining × man-advantage heuristic. Correct, non-discretionary calls (e.g. semi-automated offside rulings) aren't counted even when they disallow a goal — only judgment/VAR-review decisions are. Treat the sign and rough size as illustrative, not precise.
